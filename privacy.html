Privacy Statement

What is Score?

Score is an alternative gradebook application, made for students in the Bellevue School District. Score was created in 2018 after the launch of the Synergy gradebook. Score was originally created by Lucas Zhou, after he found it frustrating to manually calculate the effect upcoming assignments will have to his grades.

How does Score work?

Score simply passes information from the Synergy gradebook database, to you. Your grade data is then plugged into our skin and presented in a clean, clutterless, and efficient way.

Who maintains Score?

Score was originally created by Lucas Zhou, but is now developed and maintained by Elliot Gluck, McKlyde Lagnada, in addition to Lucas.

How does Score protect my data?

User security and data integrity is of the utmost concern to us. Score protects userdata in a number of ways, including utilizing the latest TLS encryption technology with strong ciphers from our servers to your computer. Also, because your data is never saved on our servers, it is virtually impossible for you to be affected in the rare event of a security breach or compromised system.

Does score sell my data?

No. Score does not sell any userdata.

How can I contact Score?

If you would like to contact the team building Score, you may reach out to us by email at team@bsdscore.com
